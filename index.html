About TechANNza

The Department of Computer Applications Yantra Club proudly present TechANNza 2k25, an Intercollegiate IT Fest designed to foster innovation, collaboration, and technical excellence. This edition features eight diverse events spanning both technical and non-technical domains, providing participants with a platform to showcase their skills and creativity. All participants will receive certificates, with winners earning trophies. The Overall Championship Trophy awaits the highest achievers. Join us on 8th April 2025, for a day of competition, learning, and the limitless potential of technology.
